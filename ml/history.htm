The 2000 ML430 SUV


History

One rainy Monday, I went to the movies and there it was- a beautiful, ML430. I wasn't sure I liked the color though. My opinion of the truck soon changed once I saw all the options that it had on it. That along with the price made the car perfect. So I wrote down the number.

Then I decided that I didn't like the color and got rid of the number before it bothered me.

A few days later, I was going to Costco and I saw this white M-Class. I thought it looked pretty good, particulary the rims which seemed bigger than usual. Upon closer examination, I realized that I was eyeing the one I saw for sale at the movies on Monday. I decided that I wanted to take a closer look so I wrote down the number...again.

I wrote it down wrong.

After hours of failed attempts to call every possible variation of the phone number, I found the ad online. I got the number and called to get more information. Sure, information, I bought the car. After a little work, Alice (yes, that's the name of the car) has become arguably the best looking truck on the road.
